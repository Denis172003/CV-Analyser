Assistant Professor - Computer Science
State University of Technology
Austin, TX | Full-time Faculty

About the Department:
The Department of Computer Science at State University of Technology is a rapidly growing department with 45 faculty members and over 2,000 students. We are committed to excellence in research, teaching, and service, with particular strengths in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and software engineering.

Position Overview:
We invite applications for a tenure-track Assistant Professor position in Computer Science, with preference for candidates specializing in machine learning, data science, or artificial intelligence. The successful candidate will contribute to our growing AI research program and teach undergraduate and graduate courses.

Primary Responsibilities:
- Conduct independent research leading to publications in top-tier venues
- Develop and teach undergraduate and graduate courses in computer science
- Supervise graduate students and mentor undergraduate researchers
- Seek external funding through grants and research proposals
- Participate in departmental and university service activities
- Collaborate with faculty across disciplines on interdisciplinary research
- Contribute to curriculum development and program improvement
- Engage with industry partners and the broader research community
- Maintain active participation in professional organizations and conferences

Research Expectations:
- Establish an independent, externally funded research program
- Publish high-quality research in peer-reviewed journals and conferences
- Present research findings at national and international conferences
- Collaborate with colleagues on interdisciplinary research projects
- Mentor graduate students and postdoctoral researchers
- Contribute to the department's research reputation and visibility

Teaching Responsibilities:
- Teach 2-3 courses per semester (standard 2-2 load with course releases)
- Develop new courses in areas of expertise
- Supervise undergraduate capstone projects and independent studies
- Mentor graduate students in research and dissertation work
- Participate in comprehensive exams and dissertation committees
- Contribute to curriculum development and assessment

Required Qualifications:
- Ph.D. in Computer Science or closely related field
- Strong research record with publications in high-quality venues
- Demonstrated expertise in machine learning, AI, or data science
- Evidence of potential for independent research and external funding
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion in higher education
- Ability to work collaboratively with faculty, students, and staff
- Teaching experience at the university level

Preferred Qualifications:
- Postdoctoral research experience
- Experience with interdisciplinary research collaborations
