Digital Marketing Manager - Growth & Performance
GrowthLab Marketing

About GrowthLab Marketing:

Position Overview:
We're seeking a results-driven Digital Marketing Manager to lead growth marketing initiatives for our diverse client portfolio. You'll develop and execute comprehensive digital marketing strategies across multiple channels to drive customer acquisition and revenue growth.

Core Responsibilities:

- Present campaign results and strategic recommendations to clients
- Mentor junior marketing team members and contribute to best practices
