Business Analyst - Process Optimization
EfficiencyFirst Consulting
Chicago, IL | Hybrid

About EfficiencyFirst Consulting:
We're a management consulting firm specializing in business process optimization and digital transformation for mid-market companies. Our analytical approach has helped clients reduce operational costs by 25% while improving productivity and customer satisfaction.

Role Summary:
We're seeking a detail-oriented Business Analyst to join our Process Optimization team. You'll work directly with client organizations to analyze business processes, identify improvement opportunities, and implement solutions that drive operational efficiency.

Primary Responsibilities:
- Conduct comprehensive business process analysis and documentation
- Gather and analyze business requirements from stakeholders across organizations
- Create detailed process maps, workflows, and system documentation
- Identify inefficiencies and recommend process improvements
- Develop business cases and ROI analyses for proposed solutions
- Facilitate workshops and stakeholder meetings to gather requirements
- Collaborate with IT teams on system implementations and integrations
- Create and maintain project documentation and status reports
- Support change management initiatives and user training programs
- Monitor and measure the success of implemented solutions

- Advanced Excel skills including pivot tables, VLOOKUP, and data analysis
- Experience with project management methodologies (Agile, Waterfall)
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects simultaneously
