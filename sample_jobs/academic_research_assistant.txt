Research Assistant - Machine Learning & AI
University of California, Berkeley
Berkeley, CA | On-campus

About the Position:
The Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at UC Berkeley is seeking a dedicated Research Assistant to support cutting-edge research in machine learning and artificial intelligence. This position offers the opportunity to work with world-renowned faculty on projects that advance the state of the art in AI.

Research Focus Areas:
Our lab focuses on developing novel machine learning algorithms with applications in computer vision, natural language processing, and robotics. Current projects include deep reinforcement learning, neural architecture search, and interpretable AI systems.

Key Responsibilities:
- Conduct literature reviews and analyze current research in machine learning and AI
- Implement and test machine learning algorithms using Python and deep learning frameworks
- Design and execute experiments to validate research hypotheses
- Collect, clean, and analyze large datasets for training and evaluation
- Collaborate with PhD students and postdocs on research projects
- Prepare research papers, conference presentations, and technical reports
- Maintain research code repositories and documentation
- Assist with grant proposal writing and research funding applications
- Present research findings at lab meetings and academic conferences
- Support undergraduate student researchers and lab operations
