Senior Product Manager - Mobile Applications
InnovateTech Inc.
Seattle, WA | Hybrid

About InnovateTech Inc.:
We're a leading mobile technology company with over 10 million active users across our suite of productivity applications. Our mission is to simplify complex workflows through intuitive mobile experiences.

Role Overview:
We're seeking a Senior Product Manager to lead our mobile application portfolio. You'll drive product strategy, work closely with engineering and design teams, and ensure we deliver exceptional user experiences that drive business growth.

Key Responsibilities:
- Define and execute product roadmap for mobile applications
- Conduct market research and competitive analysis to identify opportunities
- Collaborate with UX/UI designers to create user-centered product experiences
- Work with engineering teams to prioritize features and manage development cycles
- Analyze user data and metrics to inform product decisions
- Lead cross-functional teams through product development lifecycle
- Communicate product vision and strategy to stakeholders and executives
- Manage product launches and go-to-market strategies
- Gather and prioritize customer feedback and feature requests
- Define and track key performance indicators (KPIs) for product success

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Engineering, Computer Science, or related field
- 5+ years of product management experience, preferably in mobile applications
- Strong analytical skills with experience in data-driven decision making
- Excellent communication and presentation skills
- Experience with Agile development methodologies
- Understanding of mobile app development processes (iOS and Android)
- Proficiency with product management tools (JIRA, Confluence, Figma)
- Experience with analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude)
- Strong project management and organizational skills
- Customer-focused mindset with user experience expertise

Preferred Qualifications:
- MBA or advanced degree in relevant field
- Experience in B2C mobile applications with large user bases
- Knowledge of app store optimization (ASO) strategies
- Experience with A/B testing and experimentation frameworks
- Understanding of mobile monetization strategies
- Technical background or ability to work closely with engineering teams
- Experience with design thinking and user research methodologies
- Knowledge of machine learning and AI applications in mobile products
- Previous startup or high-growth company experience
- International product launch experience

Technical Skills:
