Laboratory Coordinator - Biomedical Engineering
Medical Research Institute
Boston, MA | Full-time

About the Institute:
The Medical Research Institute is a leading biomedical research facility affiliated with Harvard Medical School and Massachusetts General Hospital. Our interdisciplinary research focuses on developing innovative medical technologies and therapeutic solutions for complex diseases.

Position Summary:
We are seeking an experienced Laboratory Coordinator to manage daily operations of our Biomedical Engineering Research Laboratory. This role involves overseeing multiple research projects, managing laboratory resources, and ensuring compliance with safety and regulatory requirements.

Core Responsibilities:
- Coordinate daily laboratory operations and research activities
- Manage laboratory inventory, equipment, and supply procurement
- Ensure compliance with safety protocols and regulatory requirements
- Train researchers on laboratory procedures and equipment operation
- Maintain detailed records of experiments, protocols, and results
- Coordinate with multiple research teams and principal investigators
- Oversee laboratory budget and expense tracking
- Manage laboratory scheduling and resource allocation
- Supervise undergraduate and graduate student researchers
- Assist with grant applications and research proposal preparation

Laboratory Management:
- Maintain and calibrate sophisticated research equipment
- Develop and implement standard operating procedures (SOPs)
- Ensure proper storage and handling of biological materials and chemicals
- Coordinate equipment maintenance and repair services
- Manage laboratory space allocation and organization
- Oversee waste disposal and environmental safety compliance
- Maintain laboratory databases and information systems
